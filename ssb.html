Five day flow

What happens at the board.

Stage 1 + Stage 2
Day 1

Screening

Officer Intelligence Rating and PPDT decide who moves into Stage 2.

Day 2

Psychology

TAT, WAT, SRT and self description reveal patterns in thought and response.

Day 3-4

GTO tasks

Group discussion, planning, obstacles, command task and lecturette.

Day 4

Personal interview

PIQ form, background, academics, current affairs and self-awareness.

Day 5

Conference

All assessors compare evidence and announce the final result.
