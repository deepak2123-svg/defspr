Physical and medical

Start correcting risks before the board finds them.

Keep the standards visible while you train. This page is a preparation aid; the medical board and the latest official notification always decide.

Army

General male minimum height is commonly listed around 157.5 cm, with relaxations for specific regions.

Navy

Height, weight and chest expansion vary by entry and branch. Keep branch-specific notification notes handy.

Air Force

Flying branch uses stricter anthropometric standards including sitting height and leg length.

Army and ground duty

Spectacles may be accepted within stated limits, depending on entry and branch.

Air Force flying

Expect the strictest unaided vision and colour perception requirements.

Navy

Executive and technical entries often differ. Check the active entry notification.

ENT

Hearing, chronic ear infection, severe DNS and sinus issues can become preventable blockers.

Dental

Plan dental work early. Dental points below the required standard can delay or stop selection.

Orthopaedic

Flat feet, knock knees, old fractures and spine concerns deserve early professional evaluation.

Often correctable

Weight, dental issues, mild flat feet, severe DNS and some varicose vein issues if handled early.

Hard to correct

Colour blindness, vision beyond limits, prohibited tattoos, significant hearing loss and cardiac anomalies.
